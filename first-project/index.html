About Us

We're excited to open up shop this year and share with Sacramento our love for motorcycles. Motorcycles are a great way to get around town and explore the city's lively districts. Rent one of our comfortable scooters or ride in style with one of our modern classics and see all of the state capitol's landmarks and experience Sacramento's numerous eateries, craft beer taphouses, and lively entertainment hubs. If you're looking for more of an adventure experience, we offer ADV motorcycles. Some of the best dirt trails can be explored 30 minutes away in Placer County were you can fish and ride alongside the beauties of the American River.
